\ud83d\udd27 Technical Architecture

Data Flow

Data Collection \u2192 Verification \u2192 Metrics Calculation \u2192 Reporting
     \u2193               \u2193               \u2193                \u2193
  Privacy Filter \u2192 Confidence Scoring \u2192 Analytics \u2192 Dashboards


Core Metrics
• **Engagement Rate**: (likes + comments) / followers
• **Footprint Score**: \u03a3(log(1 + followers) \u00d7 platform_weight)
• **Business Presence Index**: Sum of business indicators (0-5)
• **Integration Score**: Cross-platform linking consistency


Verification Signals
• **High Confidence**: Business registration, press features, consistent bio
• **Medium Confidence**: Multiple geotags, local collaborations
• **Low Confidence**: Single mentions, sporadic references
